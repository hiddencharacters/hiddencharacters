ABOUT HIDDEN CHARACTERS


Hidden Characters is the creative collaboration of Dániel Nagy and Péter Orbán. Our office is located in a friendly showroom and workplace of fellow designers called Flatlab, in the heart of Budapest.

The goal of our cooperation is to make personalized, innovative and first quality works in Hungary, and around the world. We are mainly focusing on identity design.
